About SafeZone
Empowering safe exploration for everyone

SafeZone is an AI-powered platform designed to help travellers, citizens, and daily commuters discover safe locations in real time. Whether you're exploring a new city or navigating familiar streets, SafeZone provides data-driven safety scores, curated recommendations, and instant emergency tools so you can move confidently. Our platform aggregates data from local authorities, community reports, and verified sources to give you the most accurate safety picture available.
