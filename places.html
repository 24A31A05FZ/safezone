⚓ Coastal City

Kakinada

A thriving port city on the Bay of Bengal, Kakinada is known for its bustling harbour, pristine beaches, and the famous Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary — home to India's second-largest mangrove forest.

🌊 River City

Rajahmundry

Nestled on the banks of the sacred Godavari river, Rajahmundry is the cultural capital of Andhra Pradesh. Famous for the Dowleswaram Barrage, scenic river ghats, and the breathtaking Papikondalu gorge boat rides.

🌊 Godavari 🛶 Boat Rides 🏛 Culture
🏰 Metropolitan

Hyderabad

The City of Pearls — Hyderabad blends Mughal heritage with a booming IT industry. From the iconic Charminar and Golconda Fort to the bustling HITEC City, it is a city of extraordinary contrasts and world-famous Hyderabadi biryani.
